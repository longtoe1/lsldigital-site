Cookies Policy

This page explains how LSL Digital may use cookies and similar technologies on this website. It is a template and should be reviewed and updated once you decide which analytics or tracking tools you use.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites function, remember preferences and understand how visitors use the site.

How we may use cookies

This site may use:

  • Essential cookies – required for basic site functionality and security.
  • Analytics cookies – to understand how visitors use the site and improve content and performance.

Managing cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings, including blocking or deleting them. If you choose to disable cookies, some parts of this website may not function correctly.

Third‑party services

If we use third‑party analytics (such as Google Analytics) or embedded content, those providers may set their own cookies. Their use of cookies is governed by their own privacy and cookies policies.
