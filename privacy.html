Privacy Policy

This page provides a simple overview of how LSL Digital handles personal data collected via this website. It is a template and should be reviewed with your own legal advisor to ensure it meets your specific needs.

What information we collect

When you contact us via email or the contact form, we may collect your name, email address, company name and any other details you choose to provide.

How we use your information

We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry, provide proposals or deliver services you have asked us to provide. We do not sell your data to third parties.

How long we keep your information

We retain enquiry information for as long as necessary to handle your request and maintain appropriate business records. If you become a client, your data may be stored in our project and billing records in line with legal and regulatory requirements.

Cookies and analytics

This site may use basic analytics or session cookies to understand how visitors use the site and to keep the site secure. See the Cookies Policy for more detail.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal data we hold about you, where applicable. To do so, please contact us using the details on the contact page.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please email chris@lsldigital.co.uk.
